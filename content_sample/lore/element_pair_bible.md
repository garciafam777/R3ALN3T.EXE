## Element Profiles

### Fire
**Tactical Role:** High-burst thermal executor. Fire saturates the grid with expanding heat zones that punish clustered formations and deny area control. Its damage-over-time ticks scale with ambient temperature, making it the premier choice for breach-and-clear operations in volatile sectors.  
**Career Affinity:** **Military Analyst** — specialists who model thermodynamic battlefield projection for strategic command.

### Aqua
**Tactical Role:** Fluid-control anchor. Aqua manipulates hydrostatic pressure across the tactical grid, creating choke-point barriers and healing currents that sustain allied units. Its efficacy spikes in high-humidity and submerged theaters, where conductivity and flow dynamics multiply its reach.  
**Career Affinity:** **Fluidics Engineer** — architects of pressurized coolant loops and atmospheric reclamation systems.

### Elec
**Tactical Role:** Arc-chain disruptor. Elec propagates ionization trails that jump between targets, shredding shielded networks and exposing backdoor vulnerabilities in enemy firmware. Grid influence centers on node domination — seizing relay points to cascade overload across sectors.  
**Career Affinity:** **Penetration Tester** — operators who stress-test hardened architectures by weaponizing electromagnetic intrusion.

### Wood
**Tactical Role:** Adaptive biomass weaver. Wood seeds the grid with rapid-growth lattices that entangle hostiles, regenerate friendly cover, and rewrite terrain passability in real time. Its tactical value compounds in organics-rich zones where gene-sequence resonance accelerates propagation.  
**Career Affinity:** **Geneticist** — designers of combat-grade flora and synthetic symbiotes for territorial denial.

### Wind
**Tactical Role:** Velocity manipulator. Wind imposes directional force vectors on the grid, displacing units, redirecting projectiles, and granting mobility superiority to allied formations. Shear-layer stacking creates invisible corridors that only Wind-attuned operators can navigate reliably.  
**Career Affinity:** **Aerodynamics Engineer** — calculators of laminar-flow combat corridors and ordnance drift compensation.

### Earth
**Tactical Role:** Immutable bulwark. Earth raises instantaneous geological formations — walls, spikes, seismic dampeners — that lock down grid sectors and absorb kinetic payloads. Its influence is absolute in bedrock-stable zones; in unstable crust it risks friendly-fire backlash.  
**Career Affinity:** **Geotechnical Engineer** — surveyors who map substrate integrity for forward operating base emplacement.

### Light
**Tactical Role:** Radiant adjudicator. Light projects hard-photon lances that ignore conventional armor, while its revelation fields strip stealth and cloaking from the grid. Doctrine dictates precision over saturation — each photon packet carries forensic metadata for post-engagement audit.  
**Career Affinity:** **Ethics Commissioner** — arbitrators who enforce rules-of-engagement compliance via photon-trace accountability logs.

### Dark
**Tactical Role:** Absorptive infiltrator. Dark folds the grid into shadow-pockets where detection fails and retaliation misfires. It feeds on suppressed signatures, converting enemy stealth into empowerment stacks. High-risk, high-reward; doctrine restricts deployment to deniable assets.  
**Career Affinity:** **Blackhat Broker** — facilitators of zero-day exploits and unattributable grid incursions.

### Null
**Tactical Role:** Void anchor. Null projects anti-resonance fields that cancel active abilities, suppress elemental scaling, and enforce a "physics-only" engagement envelope. Its presence on the grid is a strategic equalizer — negating both enemy and friendly supernatural advantages.  
**Career Affinity:** **Quantum Cryptographer** — architects of encryption that survives post-quantum decryption via vacuum-state key exchange.

### Glitch
**Tactical Role:** Corruption vector. Glitch injects non-deterministic entropy into the grid, scrambling targeting logic, inverting damage types, and spawning phantom contacts. Unpredictable by design — Glitch operators accept collateral chaos as the price of asymmetric disruption.  
**Career Affinity:** **(Rogue)** — no sanctioned career track; Glitch affinity manifests in unregistered operators who survive kernel-space corruption events.

### Plasma
**Tactical Role:** Fusion lance. Plasma channels magnetically confined stellar-grade matter, delivering armor-piercing thermal lances that bypass conventional heat dissipation. Grid influence radiates electromagnetic interference, degrading enemy targeting within the containment envelope.  
**Career Affinity:** **Fusion Engineer** — maintainers of tokamak-grade reactor cores that power capital-grid installations.

### Crystal
**Tactical Role:** Lattice fortifier. Crystal grows refractive structures that split, redirect, and amplify beam-type attacks while granting ablative shielding to allies. Its grid presence creates "facet zones" where angles of incidence dictate tactical outcomes — geometry as weapon.  
**Career Affinity:** **Material Scientist** — developers of meta-crystalline composites for hull plating and directed-energy optics.

### Time
**Tactical Role:** Chrono-manipulator. Time applies local dilation fields — accelerating ally cooldowns, stalling enemy projectiles, rewinding micro-trajectories. Grid influence is temporal rather than spatial; mastery requires predicting causality branches three turns deep.  
**Career Affinity:** **Quantum Physicist** — theorists who model closed-timelike-curve combat simulations for strategic forecasting.

### Gravity
**Tactical Role:** Mass anchor. Gravity warps the grid's geodesic topology, pulling clusters into kill zones, flinging projectiles off-course, and pinning high-mobility targets. Its scaling peaks near orbital tethers and mass-driver corridors where spacetime curvature is already engineered.  
**Career Affinity:** **Aerospace Engineer** — designers of gravity-assist launch corridors and orbital debris mitigation grids.

### Sound
**Tactical Role:** Resonance architect. Sound weaponizes constructive and destructive interference — shattering crystalline armor, stunning via infrasound, and encoding data in carrier waves that penetrate Faraday sectors. Grid influence propagates through any medium; vacuum is its only hard counter.  
**Career Affinity:** **Acoustic Designer** — engineers of sonic camouflage, seismic comms, and resonance-based structural health monitoring.

### Metal
**Tactical Role:** Conduit forge. Metal reshapes the grid's conductive pathways, erecting instant fortifications, redirecting energy weapons, and fabricating ammunition from scrap nodes. Its tactical tempo is industrial — slow to initialize, overwhelming once the supply chain engages.  
**Career Affinity:** **Construction Engineer** — supervisors of rapid-deployment prefab habitats and battlefield logistics pipelines.

### Poison
**Tactical Role:** Attrition vector. Poison seeds persistent contamination fields that bypass shields, stack across turns, and transmit between adjacent units. Grid influence is insidious — no visual indicator until threshold breach; counterplay requires dedicated purging protocols.  
**Career Affinity:** **Toxicologist** — analysts who catalog pathogen dispersal models and synthesize targeted antidotes for grid-scale contamination events.

### Nature
**Tactical Role:** Regenerative ecosystem. Nature establishes self-sustaining biomes on the grid — healing allies, spawning neutral fauna that disrupt enemy pathing, and terraforming hostile sectors into friendly terrain. Its power compounds exponentially when left uncontested.  
**Career Affinity:** **Synthetic Biologist** — architects of engineered ecosystems that serve as both life-support and area-denial infrastructure.

## Doctrine Note

> *No element exists in isolation. The nine pairs form a closed topological loop — each advantage creates a corresponding vulnerability. Mastery lies not in maximizing one element, but in reading the grid's elemental weather and rotating affinity to maintain perpetual advantage. The operator who chains pairs correctly does not fight the enemy; they fight the enemy's options.*
