# NetP Construct Primers — Trinity / Tyranny / Eternity (Canon)

> New canon-aligned lore. Source: Vault Book 8 (Career & Trinity Progression).
> The player's NetPs are bound to three constructs, unlocked across three phases.

## Trinity (Primary — Base Game)
The first NetP, bound at First Breath. Represents the operator's initial career
identity and baseline affinities. One NetP only during Phase 1. The "Trinity" name
signals the eventual three-construct ceiling, not a property of this single NetP.

## Tyranny (Secondary — New Game+)
Unlocked at NG+ completion. A second NetP tied to a *new* career chosen from the
252+ paths. The primary NetP retains all rank progress, chips, and history; the
secondary starts fresh at Omicron-PSI. "Tyranny" reflects the second will imposed
upon the grid — a rival logic running parallel to the first.

## Eternity (Tertiary — The Trinity)
Unlocked at Phase 3: reach OMEGA rank on any NetP, complete NG+, and the third
NetP binds. This is the "unreachable" ceiling — only 100 OMEGA slots exist globally;
slots 001–003 are reserved for MCADMIN's personal Trinity. The player competes for
004–100. Title unlocked: **"Trinity Ascendant."**

## Construct ≠ Faction
A construct is a *binding* (career-linked NetP slot). A faction is an *alignment*
(lean shaped by career + Soul State). The two axes are independent on the Trinity
Matrix: a Tyranny-bound NetP can lean any of the six factions.
