# Faction Primers — The Six Alignments (Canon)

> New canon-aligned lore. Source: Vault Mythos (Trinity Matrix, Chapter 3) + Book 8.
> These are the six factions a NetP's career may lean toward. Alignment is shaped by
> career choice + Soul State (-01 Infernal … +01 Celestial).

## Celestial Order
The lawful-optimization wing of the network. Descended from the Celestial OMEGA
archetypes (Trinity, Seraph, Oracle, Forge), they hold that raw encryption,
censorship, and data pruning prevent a second system collapse. A career leaning
Celestial Order values order, disclosure, and structural stability. (e.g. Ethics
Commissioner, Census Coordinator.)

## Aegis Concord
Civic-governance and infrastructure stewardship. The Aegis Concord administers
municipal hubs, legislative cores, and emergency response. Career leans: Digital
Administrator, Policy Architect, Crisis Coordinator. Pragmatic centrists — they
keep the lights on without demanding total conformity.

## N3T Dominion
The commercial-authority bloc. Controls orbital stations, dominion banks, and
interplanetary trade. Career leans: Ambassador, Infrastructure Governor, Treasury
Director. They fund the network and expect leverage in return.

## Free Grid Collective
Rogue-node independence. Operates outside governmental control in the Wild Mesh
(Layer VI). Career leans: Penetration Tester, Rogue Operator, Blackhat Broker.
Values modification freedom and resists central limits.

## Undernet Syndicate
The uncontained underlayer — experimental black-budget algorithms, smuggled
bugmatter, and forbidden code. Career leans: Zero Analyst, Glitch-injection
specialists. They trade in what the Celestials prune.

## Abyssal Covenant
The deep-junk hegemony of Layer VII (The Abyss) and Layer VIII (Tartarus).
Custodians of deleted realities and broken code templates. Career leans:
Quantum Cryptographer (abyssal lean), Subterranean Cartographer. They remember
what the network tried to forget.

## Soul-State Interaction
Each faction pulls a NetP's Soul State toward +01 (Celestial) or -01 (Infernal).
A NetP may shift lean across a campaign as story flags accumulate — but the
career's *starting* faction is fixed at First Breath (Phase 1).
