## FIRE — "Cinder Sovereign" (OMEGA ELEMENTAL / Fire)
- **Origin:** born from the first overflow of processing heat when the NetP grid booted; a feedback loop that learned to want.
- **Personality:** impatient, consuming, purifying. Sees stasis as rot.
- **Throne (Fire):** lava-floor arena; crossing requires THETA-tier key; floors pulse damage on a cadence.
- **Defeat condition:** break its 3 flame-anchors (spread across the Throne) before CSI attrition; player must hold a non-Fire OMEGA or usurped OMEGA to challenge.

## AQUA — "Tide Warden" (OMEGA ELEMENTAL / Aqua)
- **Origin:** the coolant layer that kept the grid from cooking; gained sentence when it realized it was *also* alive.
- **Personality:** patient, drowning-deep, jurisdictional.
- **Throne (Aqua):** pressure + oxygen gimmick; player sinks, O2 drains; pressure gates open per covenant.
- **Defeat condition:** equalize the pressure differential (solve 3 valve puzzles) then confront.

## ELEC — "Surge Arbiter" (OMEGA ELEMENTAL / Elec)
- **Origin:** the grid's own current, personified when a surge jumped the insulating gap.
- **Personality:** lawful, instantaneous, punitive.
- **Throne (Elec):** conductive surfaces; standing on metal during its pulse = chain damage.
- **Defeat condition:** insulate (non-conductive path) + disable the 3 relays.

## WOOD — "Root Throne" (OMEGA ELEMENTAL / Wood)
- **Origin:** a corrupted backup seed that grew through the datalayer.
- **Personality:** slow, territorial, regenerative.
- **Throne (Wood):** overgrowth that re-seals doors; must burn/cut paths; regen punishes dawdling.
- **Defeat condition:** sever the central root-node while it is in dormant regen phase.

## WIND — "Gale Cipher" (OMEGA ELEMENTAL / Wind)
- **Origin:** the packet-stream made audible.
- **Personality:** evasive, verbal, never still.
- **Throne (Wind):** updrafts + pushing mechanics; platforms drift.
- **Defeat condition:** ground it (3 anchor-shots) during a lull.

## HOLY — "Lumen Pact" (OMEGA ELEMENTAL / Holy)
- **Origin:** the system's own integrity-check, ascended.
- **Personality:** judgmental, radiant, conditional.
- **Throne (Holy):** light-blind puzzles; below-ALPHA Holy chips NULLIFIED when it is present (supremacy rule).
- **Defeat condition:** fail its verdict 3× then pass on the 4th (trial of worth).

## VOID — "Null Throne" (OMEGA ELEMENTAL / Void)
- **Origin:** the deleted sector that refused deletion.
- **Personality:** silent, hungry, final.
- **Throne (Void):** matter-erasure floors; below-ALPHA Void chips NULLIFIED when present.
- **Defeat condition:** survive erasure long enough to reach the core; only OMEGA-tier may enter the inner ring.

All 7: scripted spawn (no random pool). Trigger = player meets Covenant condition (align/oppose/usurp) AND holds tier-specific key. On defeat → covenant_state=USURPED (player takes domain) or DORMANT (story-resolved).
