# R3ALN3T.EXE — Undernet Roster
## Mischievous NPCs (Tier-Ranked Only, No Elemental Layer)

---

## Design Philosophy

The Undernet Syndicate is one of your six factions (per the character-card generator), and unlike the Angelic/Demonic/Mythos/Cosmos rosters, these are framed as **NPCs first, combatants second** — hackers, tricksters, small-time con artists and information brokers living in SkyLand's back-alley data-space. Visual language: scrappy, patchwork, graffiti-tagged plate armor scavenged and mismatched rather than uniform like NetP's clean design or the corrupted antagonists' consistent ladder. Nothing here should look "official" — every unit should look like it was assembled from spare parts and personality.

This roster uses **only the 24-tier Greek rank system** from your generator (OMEGA highest → PSI lowest) — no Element cross-table like the NetP Elemental doc, per your direction. One named NPC per tier, 24 total, each with a distinct mischievous personality hook alongside the visual spec, since these are meant to function as recurring/memorable NPCs rather than anonymous grunt-family fodder.

**Shared palette anchor:** sickly neon-green (#9AFF4A) and deep graffiti-purple (#4A1A6A) as the two accent colors threaded through the whole roster — distinct from every other faction's palette (doesn't overlap NetP's cyan, the corruption ladder's red-to-cyan run, Angelic's cool white, Demonic's oxidized red, or the Mythos/Cosmos hex sets), so Undernet units read as an unmistakably separate "flavor" the instant they appear.

**Shared silhouette rule:** every unit wears at least one piece of visibly stolen/mismatched gear — a plate segment that doesn't match the rest of their kit, a NetP-style vambrace clearly scavenged rather than issued. Small detail, big personality payoff.

## Roster Totals
- **24 unique Undernet NPCs**, one per Greek tier, no boss-tier escalation beyond OMEGA (Fiddlecode) — deliberately kept flatter/friendlier than the antagonist/Angelic/Demonic rosters, since these are meant to populate hub areas and side content rather than anchor combat encounters.

---

## Production Notes
- These work best as **dialogue-bearing hub NPCs** rather than combat units — recommend light barks/idle dialogue per personality hook above rather than full boss-style movesets.
- Patchwork/stolen-gear rule is cheap to implement: reuse existing plate assets from other rosters (a stray Angelic vambrace, a Demonic shoulder-plate, an antagonist grunt piece) recolored slightly off — turns this roster into essentially a "clearance rack" of your existing asset library rather than requiring new geometry.
- OMEGA (Fiddlecode) is the only unit here suggested as a possible light combat/chase encounter if you want one Undernet "boss-adjacent" beat — everyone else is intended as pure flavor/hub population.
