# R3ALN3T.EXE — Demonic Roster
## 3D Realistic Character Specs (Type: Demonic, per character-card generator)

---

## Design Philosophy

Demonic-type entities use the **Abyssal Covenant** and **Undernet Syndicate** factions from your generator script. Visual mirror-opposite of the Angelic roster: where Angelic is vertical/symmetrical/cold-white/steady-glow, Demonic is hunched/asymmetrical/deep-red-black/flickering-glow. Distinct from the antagonist corruption ladder (Acts I–IV) — Demonic units aren't "corrupted data," they're a separate faction entirely, so they don't use any hex value already claimed by KERN.EXE, MIRROR.EXE, or SOVEREIGN.NULL.

Shared base: deep crimson-black (#1A0A0C base, #D6283F is already used by Act I's KERN.EXE — Demonic instead anchors on **#8A0F1F**, a darker, more oxidized red, keeping the two "red" factions visually distinguishable).

## PART 1 — MAJOR BOSSES (Demonic)

### Boss 1: MALEBRANCHE-01 "The Undertow" (Demonic primary/OMEGA-tier)

| Metric | Spec |
|---|---|
| Height | 2.35 m |
| Build | Hunched, broad, asymmetrical — left arm oversized/clawed, right arm normal proportion (direct visual opposite of SERAPH-01's perfect symmetry) |
| Proportions | 6.5 heads (stockiest ratio in the roster — reads as brutish rather than elegant) |

**Face:** No visible eyes at all — a smooth featureless lower face with a single horizontal emissive slit (#FF2A3F) where a mouth would be. Deliberately withholds the "eyes as readable expression" tool used by every other major character.

**Horns/crown-equivalent:** Six curved, asymmetric horn-spikes (parallel to SERAPH-01's six wing-panels) — jagged and irregular rather than geometric/clean.

**Costume:** Fused plate-and-flesh silhouette — armor doesn't sit cleanly on top of a suit like NetP/Angelic units; it reads as partially grown from the body. Base tone **oxidized crimson-black (#1A0A0C)**, plate sections in **dark iron (#2E1A1C)**, emissive cracks in **#FF2A3F**, flickering irregularly (never steady — direct opposite of Angelic's constant glow rule).

**Materials:** High roughness throughout (~0.7) — nothing about this design should read as sleek or polished; the whole point is "unrefined and violent" versus Angelic's "precise and clean."

## UE5 Production Notes
- Same 2 shared skeletons as the Angelic roster (humanoid + quadruped) — Angelic and Demonic can share rig topology entirely, only materials/proportions/asymmetry differ, keeping both 52-entry rosters cheap to animate as a pair.
- All emissive behavior in this roster is **irregular/flickering, never steady** — deliberate inverse of the Angelic roster's constant-glow rule, giving players an instant faction read from lighting behavior alone before they even see color or silhouette.
- Recommend reusing Angelic's Family C/D mesh bases with a "corruption/rot" material swap rather than new geometry — the structural parallel between the two rosters (Choir↔Fiends, Wardens↔Hounds, Motes↔Motes, Vessels↔Carcasses, Ledgerbound↔Pitbound) was built specifically to make this possible.
